About Us

iGaming Licensing is a leading consultancy firm specialising in iGaming licensing and regulatory compliance support, delivering tailored B2B and B2C online gaming licence solutions to operators and software suppliers worldwide.

Our team comprises some of the industry’s most experienced iGaming regulatory experts, with a proven track record across Tier One and regulated jurisdictions. We bring together decades of hands-on knowledge and strategic insight, helping our clients secure licences efficiently, maintain rigorous compliance and thrive in competitive global markets.

iGaming Licensing also recognises that not every operator and software supplier requires a “tier one” licence. However, we apply tier one standards across all client compliance frameworks. By doing so, operators, whether large or small, can ensure their operations are built on solid foundations, providing long-term stability, security and confidence for their partners and players.

Whether you are a start-up seeking your first online gambling licence or an established brand expanding into new regulated markets, we offer strategic support every step of the way.
